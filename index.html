Hallo! Welcome to the ultimate Causa Rellena tutorial,made by a peruvian for not so peruvians! Causa rellena is a delicious and colorful Peruvian dish made from layers of mashed yellow potatoes and various fillings. Let's get into it!

Ingredients

  • Potato Layer (Mashed Yellow Potatoes)
  • 3 tablespoons aji amarillo (yellow chili pepper) paste
  • Juice of 4 small lemons (Or 2 Limes)
  • Fillings (shredded chicken, tuna, avocado, or even seafood.)
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon mustard
  • 2 hard-boiled eggs, sliced (MANDATORY, WE LOVE EGGS RAHH)
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Boil the Potatoes (20 mins hun)
  2. Drain the potatoes and mash them until smooth.
  3. While the potatoes are still warm, mix in the aji amarillo paste, vegetable oil, lime juice, and salt if you wish (and like salt, heh). Adjust the seasoning to taste.
  4. In a bowl or plate, combine the shredded chicken (or tuna, or seafood, or whatever your filling is) with mayonnaise, mustard, lemon juice, and if you prefer, salt, and pepper. Mix until the filling is well coated and the flavors are well blended
  5. Now onto assembling:Take half of the mashed potato mixture and spread it evenly in a serving dish or mold. Press it down firmly to create a smooth, even layer. Then add the filling, avocado and egg, and after that,spread the remaining half of the potato mixture over the filling and smooth it out.
